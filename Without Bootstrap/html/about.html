About us

What is DreamPath.lk ?

        DreamPath.lk is jobs portal in Sri Lanka that bridges job seekers and employers and deliver the best for both parties, Sri Lankan own & operated online job site in Sri Lanka.We have listed the jobs under various job categories (industry) and sub- categories, as well as we have categorized the jobs based on job location (provinces), job type (full time, part time etc), and more. DreamPath.lk makes the job hunting easy in Sri Lanka with us, by finding more jobs in Sri Lanka & very easy to application system we have.More than 50 different categories. We also carefully review all jobs that are being published, to make sure the quality is up to our standards. If you'd like to get in touch with us, go to Contact us.
